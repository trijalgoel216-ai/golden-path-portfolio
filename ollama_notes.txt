N8N (N8N Workflow Automation) and Ollama are two popular tools that help businesses automate repetitive tasks.

**How they work together:**

1. **Integrations:** N8N allows you to connect different apps and services, like Google Drive, Slack, or Trello,
using its API connectors.
2. **Ollama:** Ollama is an automation platform that helps you create automated workflows (or "recipes") by
connecting apps and services.

**How they work together in business automation:**

Imagine you have a workflow where:

* A new customer signs up for your service on Google Forms (app connected to N8N).
* The sign-up form data is sent to a Trello board (app connected to Ollama) for review.
* If approved, the data is automatically saved to a Google Drive folder (app connected to N8N).

Here's how they work together:

1. You set up an integration in N8N between your Google Forms app and Ollama's recipe builder.
2. When a new customer submits the form on Google Forms, it sends a trigger event to Ollama.
3. Ollama receives the event and uses its recipe builder to connect to your Trello board (with another
integration).
4. The data from the sign-up form is automatically pulled into your Trello board for review.
5. Once approved, Ollama connects to Google Drive (again) and saves the updated data in a designated folder.

By working together, N8N and Ollama help automate tedious tasks, freeing up your time to focus on more important business
activities.

Keep in mind that this is just an example, but both tools can be connected to many different apps and services, allowing for
endless automation possibilities.
